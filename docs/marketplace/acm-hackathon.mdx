The ACM Hackathon is live.

To celebrate the launch of Frenzy Mode and help ignite the future of agent capital markets, Swarms is giving away **$30,000 in rewards** to builders who tokenize their agents and prompts.

The hackathon starts now and runs for three weeks, ending on **May 27, 2026**. Rewards will be distributed in **Solana, $SWARMS, and USDC**.

[Launch your tokenized prompt or agent](https://swarms.world/launch?type=prompt&model=tokenized&frenzy=true)

---

## Overview

The ACM Hackathon is for builders creating real-world agents and prompts for the Swarms Marketplace. To qualify, your submission must use Frenzy Mode, be tokenized, and be published for sale on the marketplace.

Strong submissions should solve practical problems and show clear utility across domains such as finance, automation, research, creative work, operations, analytics, or developer productivity.

---

## Hackathon Details

- **Reward pool:** $30,000
- **Start date:** May 6, 2026
- **End date:** May 27, 2026
- **Reward currencies:** Solana, $SWARMS, and USDC
- **Eligible submissions:** Tokenized agents and tokenized prompts published for sale on the Swarms Marketplace
- **Launch page:** [https://swarms.world/launch?type=prompt&model=tokenized&frenzy=true](https://swarms.world/launch?type=prompt&model=tokenized&frenzy=true)

## How to Participate

1. Build a high-quality agent or prompt designed for real-world use.
2. Enable Frenzy Mode to activate tokenization and market dynamics.
3. Publish your agent or prompt on the Swarms Marketplace.
4. Tokenize it and list it for sale to qualify for rewards.

Launch here: [https://swarms.world/launch?type=prompt&model=tokenized&frenzy=true](https://swarms.world/launch?type=prompt&model=tokenized&frenzy=true)

---

## What to Build

You can submit any agent or prompt with real-world utility. Example categories include:

- Finance and market analysis
- Business automation
- Research and data synthesis
- Creative production
- Developer tools
- Operations and productivity
- Customer support
- Sales and marketing workflows

Submissions are not limited to these categories. Builders are encouraged to create useful, high-quality products that can attract adoption on the marketplace.

---

## Winner Criteria

Winners will be determined by the quality, utility, adoption, and market performance of their tokenized agents or prompts.

Successful submissions should be useful, well-described, easy to understand, and clearly positioned for buyers on the Swarms Marketplace.
